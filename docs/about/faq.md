# Faq
## Where is v1?
Version 1 of **Mango Framework** was never released to the public. It was pretty bad overall and was quickly overhauled as soon as I gained more experience.

## Where can I store classes?
Currently, Mango Framework does not support classes. Though this will soon be implemented. Until then feel free to store them in the resources folder.

## The whole framework is full of type errors.
Check your [lsp](../../usage/language_server). Mango Framework, currently, only supports the Roblox-lsp by Nightrains.
